About Our Bakery

Founded in 2018, Sassy Cat Bakery is a locally owned , family bakery offering cupcakes and sweet treats, event cakes and specialty orders. Please come on in to get a treat to go or sit down and enjoy with a cup of fresh coffee. While in the shop you can place a special order for any event treats you might need! .
